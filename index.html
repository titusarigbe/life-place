About Us
Welcome to The Redeemed Christian church Of God, The Life Place Parish, Dubai. We are a Christ-centered church dedicated to sharing the message of the Gospel, growing in our relationship with God, and living out His purpose in our daily lives.

  • Our Vision

    To make heaven
    To take as many people as possible with us.
    To have a member of the RCCG in every family of all nations

  • Our Mission

    To accomplish No.1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
    To accomplish No.2 and 3 above.
    We will plant churches within five minutes driving distance in every city and town
    We will pursue these objectives until every nations in the world is reached for JESUS CHRIST OUR LORD
